Alternate Munchkin

Unique Rules of Alternate Munchkin

  • The starting level is 3 instead of 1
  • Create a seperate deck of door cards made up of just race and class cards. Either door deck can be drawn from
  • Distribute a race and a class card to every player
  • After selecting a short, average, or long game, a random and unknown timer in that time range will be set
  • The winner is the player with the highest level at the end of the timer
  • Every player has one more turn after the timer goes off to attempt to get the highest level
